How did this all started...

Two years ago, I accepted an interview invitation in this small town, in the north of France. It was a bright, sunny day and it was my first time I was exploring alone a city in a foreign country.

Being eager to find out more about foreign cultures, I thought that this would be a good chance to improve my foreign language skills and to travel.

It is said that speaking a foreign language not only facilitates communication with the people from a different culture, but it also gives you a different perspective on your own thought process, a new light on everything that you already know. And this transforms you.
